Help: Using our Weather App

Welcome to our Weather App! This page is here to help you learn how to check the weather, save your favorite cities, and update you account settings.

1. Searching for the weather

Find the weather in any city easily:

  • Search Bar: Type the city name in the search bar.
  • View Results: See the current temperature, humidity, wind speed, and weather conditions instantly.
  • More Details: Check hourly or weekly forecasts for the city.

2. Managing favorite cities

Save and view the weather for your favorite places:

  • Add Favorites: Tap the star icon next to a city to save it.
  • View Favorites: Go to "Favorites" to see weather updates for saved cities.
  • Remove Favorites: Tap the star icon again to remove a city from your list.

3. Understanding weather details

Learn more about the weather:

  • Current Conditions: See real-time temperature, precipitation, and cloud cover.
  • Hourly Forecast: Check what the weather will be like in the next few hours.
  • Weekly Forecast: Check what the weather will be like during the week.

4. Profile and Account Management

Manage your account easily:

  • Edit Profile: Update your username, email, or password.

We hope this guide helps you use our weather app easily. Search for cities, save your favorites, and adjust settings to stay updated with the latest weather.
